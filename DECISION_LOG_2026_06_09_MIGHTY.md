# Decision Log — Mighty Identity

Date: 2026-06-09
Status: LOCKED

## Decision

Do NOT move MIGHTY toward heavy Armor-as-resource gameplay.

Reason:

Previous testing showed that combining:
- Strong attack stats
- Frequent Armor generation

creates an overpowered archetype.

Armor will remain primarily a defensive mechanic rather than a spendable resource.

## Locked Change

Replace:

Siege Volley
Cost 4
Deal 4 damage split randomly among enemies (face if board empty)

With:

Cleave
Cost 4
Deal 2 damage to two random enemy minions.

## Rationale

- Feels more Warrior-like.
- Encourages board combat.
- Reduces random face damage.
- Improves archetype identity.
- Avoids pushing burn-based gameplay.

## Future Direction

MIGHTY identity remains:
- Durable board presence
- Removal
- Combat dominance
- Armor as defense
- Stat buffs

Avoid:
- Excessive Armor scaling
- Armor-to-damage conversion
- Heavy Enrage package unless future balance testing supports it
