# Elemental Crystal TCG — Race & Tribe Structure

## Design Philosophy

The world of Elemental Crystal is populated entirely by creatures.

Humans do not exist.

Traditional fantasy races such as elves, dwarves, or orcs do not exist.

Creature identity is one of the primary pillars of the game's visual design and worldbuilding.

Each Crystal Affinity is associated with specific creature families, allowing players to instantly recognize a card's faction through silhouette, appearance, and theme.

# Neutral Creatures

Neutral cards represent creatures and entities that exist outside the three Crystal Affinities.

Neutral is not a faction.

Neutral is a collection of independent creature groups.

---

# Launch Set Identity

For the first release, faction recognition should be extremely simple.

Players should immediately associate:

* MIGHTY = Bears
* SWIFT = Cats
* VITAL = Frogs

This clear visual separation improves readability, memorability, and brand identity.

Future sets can expand each Crystal Affinity with additional creature tribes while preserving the core identity established at launch.

---

# Forbidden Concepts

The following should not appear as races, tribes, or primary creature identities:

* Humans
* Elves
* Dwarves
* Orcs
* Goblins
* Halflings
* Human professions as species

Avoid creature concepts based on:

* Clerics
* Priests
* Paladins
* Knights
* Assassins
* Warlocks

Creature identity should originate from animal families, crystal ecosystems, ancient beings, spirits, or constructs rather than traditional humanoid fantasy archetypes.
