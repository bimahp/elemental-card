# Decision Log — Vital Identity

Date: 2026-06-09
Status: LOCKED

## Direction

VITAL will move toward Warcraft Priest / Life Priest fantasy.

Primary themes:
- Healing
- Protection
- Persistence
- Spirits
- Resurrection

Avoid emphasizing:
- Mind control
- Theft identity
- Generic control removal packages

Core fantasy:

'My creatures may die, but they keep coming back.'
