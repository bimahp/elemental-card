# DESIGN_V5_ARCHETYPE_DIRECTION

## Purpose

This document supersedes the original SWIFT-only rework proposal and serves as the current unified direction for all three archetypes.

The goal is to create:

- MIGHTY = Warrior-inspired
- SWIFT = Rogue-inspired
- VITAL = Priest-inspired

while maintaining distinct gameplay identities and healthier matchup balance.

---

# Core Archetype Triangle

## MIGHTY

Wins through:
- Board combat
- Efficient removal
- Durable threats
- Stat buffs
- Armor as defense

Fantasy:
- Bears
- Cute
- Chunky
- Funny
- Frontline combat

Important design constraint:

Armor is defensive only.

Previous testing showed that combining:
- High attack output
- Frequent armor generation
- Armor-to-damage conversion

creates an overpowered archetype.

Avoid:
- Shield Slam style scaling
- Armor as a primary resource
- Heavy Enrage packages

Locked card change:

Siege Volley
→ Cleave

Cost 4
Deal 2 damage to two random enemy minions.

---

## SWIFT

Wins through:
- Tempo
- Flexibility
- Stealth
- Bounce
- Theft
- Temporary Energy generation

Fantasy:
- Cats
- Trickery
- Agility
- Ambushes
- Hit and run tactics

SWIFT bends the rules of Energy and positioning to gain short-term advantages.

The archetype should feel closer to Hearthstone Rogue than a Combo deck.

---

## VITAL

Wins through:
- Healing
- Protection
- Persistence
- Spirit generation
- Resurrection

Fantasy:
- Frogs
- Life magic
- Restoration
- Spirits
- Recovery

Core fantasy:

'My creatures may die, but they keep coming back.'

Avoid emphasizing:
- Mind control identity
- Theft identity
- Generic control-removal gameplay

---

# SWIFT Mechanics

## Ambush

Ambush: gains a bonus the first time it attacks.

Example:

Shadowblade Stalker
Cost 2
2/2
Ambush: Gain +2 Attack when attacking for the first time.

---

## Preparation

Preparation cards reduce the cost of future Actions.

Example:

Preparation
Cost 1
Your next Action costs 2 less this turn.

---

## Opportunist

Rewards efficient sequencing.

Example:

Opportunist Cutpurse
2/3
Whenever you play your second card each turn, gain 1 temporary Energy.

# Locked Vital Card Changes

## Husk Reclaimer

Remove.

Replace with:

Spirit Recall
Cost 4
Return a friendly minion from your discard pile to your hand.

Purpose:
- Supports persistence identity.
- Creates long-term value.
- Avoids removal-centric gameplay.

---

## Domination Rite

Remove.

Replace with:

Guardian Spirit
Cost 3
1/4
Taunt
Deathrattle: Heal your Core 3.

Purpose:
- Defensive spirit creature.
- Reinforces healing fantasy.
- Creates a uniquely Vital board piece.

# Design Rule

Avoid combining card draw and Energy cheating on the same card.

Historically this creates runaway combo decks and undermines the intended tempo identity of SWIFT.
