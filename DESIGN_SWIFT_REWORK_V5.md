# DESIGN_V5_ARCHETYPE_DIRECTION

## Purpose

This document supersedes the original SWIFT-only rework proposal and serves as the current unified direction for all three archetypes.

The goal is to create:

- MIGHTY = Warrior-inspired
- SWIFT = Rogue-inspired
- VITAL = Priest-inspired

while maintaining distinct gameplay identities and healthier matchup balance.

---

# Core Archetype Triangle

## MIGHTY

Wins through:
- Board combat
- Efficient removal
- Durable threats
- Stat buffs
- Armor as defense

Fantasy:
- Bears
- Cute
- Chunky
- Playful
- Frontline combat

Important design constraint:

Armor is defensive only.

Do NOT introduce Shield Slam style mechanics.
Do NOT convert Armor into damage.

Locked card change:

Siege Volley
→ Cleave

Cost 4
Deal 2 damage to two random enemy minions.

### Locked Mighty Card Names

#### Monsters
Reckless Brute → Bonk Bear

Bastion Defender → Chubby Bear

Warbound Raider → Grumpy Bear

#### Actions
Execute → Big Bonk

Shield Wall → Extra Fluffy

Cleave → Double Swipe

#### Equipment
Iron Warmaul → Bonk Hammer

Titan's Warplate → Mega Helmet

Design goal:
Cute mascot-first naming. Not epic fantasy.

---

## SWIFT

Wins through:
- Tempo
- Stealth
- Bounce
- Theft
- Temporary Energy generation

Fantasy:
- Sneaky Cats
- Clever Cats
- Agile Cats
- Opportunistic Cats

Important design direction:

Move away from Combo-focused identity.
Move toward Rogue-style tempo.

SWIFT bends the rules of Energy and positioning to gain short-term advantages.

Avoid combining card draw and Energy cheating on the same card.

### Locked Swift Naming Table

Quick Slice → Quick Scratch

Smoke Bomb → Hide and Sneak

Calculated Strike → Surprise Pounce

Rapid Reflexes → Cat Nap

Vanishing Act → Hide and Seek

Coordinated Strike → Sharp Claws

Master Thief's Gambit → Cat Burglar

Future minion naming examples:
- Sneaky Kitten
- Alley Cat
- Bush Prowler

Naming goal:
Sneaky cats.
Clever cats.
Not ninja cats.
Not meme cats.

---

## VITAL

Wins through:
- Healing
- Protection
- Persistence
- Spirit generation
- Resurrection

Fantasy:
- Frogs
- Life magic
- Restoration
- Spirits
- Recovery

Core fantasy:

'My creatures may die, but they keep coming back.'

Avoid emphasizing:
- Mind control identity
- Theft identity
- Generic control-removal gameplay

- Buffs
- Armor

## SWIFT
- Bounce
- Stealth
- Theft
- Temporary Energy

SWIFT should be the only archetype that regularly gains temporary Energy.

## VITAL
- Healing
- Protection
- Spirit generation
- Resurrection

# Locked Vital Card Changes

## Husk Reclaimer

Remove.

Replace with:

Spirit Recall
Cost 4
Return a friendly minion from your discard pile to your hand.

Purpose:
- Supports persistence identity.
- Creates long-term value.
- Avoids removal-centric gameplay.

---

## Domination Rite

Remove.

Replace with:

Guardian Spirit
Cost 3
1/4
Taunt
Deathrattle: Heal your Core 3.

Purpose:
- Defensive spirit creature.
- Reinforces healing fantasy.
- Creates a uniquely Vital board piece.

---

# Design Rule

Avoid combining card draw and Energy cheating on the same card.

Historically this creates runaway combo decks and undermines the intended tempo identity of SWIFT.
